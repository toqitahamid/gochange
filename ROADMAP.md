# Roadmap

This roadmap uses a Now / Next / Later format. Now is actively in progress. Next is scoped and planned. Later is directional — the intent is clear but timing and scope are flexible.

---

## Now

**Core workout experience polish**
- Exercise reordering within a workout day
- Concentric activity rings on the home dashboard
- Liquid Glass design system applied across all primary screens
- Rest timer accuracy improvements

**Watch app stability**
- Reliable WatchConnectivity sync on app launch
- Handle session state when Watch app is backgrounded mid-workout

---

## Next

**Progressive overload tracking**
- Per-exercise history chart showing weight and volume trends over time
- Inline overload suggestion with configurable increment rules (e.g. +2.5kg when all sets completed at top of rep range)
- Personal records detection and display during active workouts

**Workout customization**
- Superset and circuit grouping within a workout day
- Per-exercise rest timer presets saved to the exercise model
- Warm-up set support (excluded from volume calculations)

**Recovery depth**
- Muscle soreness logging per session with recovery day projection
- Week-over-week recovery trend on the home dashboard
- Training readiness score surfaced as a widget timeline entry

**Watch app independence**
- Persist recent workout templates locally on Watch for offline use
- Completed Watch sessions sync back to iPhone and appear in session history
- Watch complications for daily strain and next scheduled workout

---

## Later

**Intelligent coaching**
- Deload week detection based on cumulative strain and recovery trends
- Auto-periodization: suggest rep range and intensity adjustments across a training block
- Injury risk flags based on volume spikes and recovery patterns

**Social and sharing**
- Share a completed workout session as a formatted image
- Export a training block summary (volume, PRs, consistency) as a PDF

**Platform expansion**
- iPad layout optimized for use on a gym floor (large set logging UI)
- Apple TV app for form video playback during workouts

**Integrations**
- Import workout templates from common formats (CSV, strong app export)
- Garmin Connect and Oura Ring recovery data as alternative HealthKit sources

---

## Not planned

- Android or Wear OS support
- Cloud sync or multi-device account system
- Coach or trainer multi-user features
- Paid subscription or in-app purchases
